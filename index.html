Elisa is Milan born fashion and design writer. She moved to London in September 2014 for a foundation year at Condé Nast College of Fashion and Design. Since then she has interned at J.J. Martins LaDoubleJ.com, Nowness, Wallpaper* and British Vogue. Her work has been published by Wallpaper* Magazine and she contributes to Corriere Della Serra's Design Magazine, Living. She is now currently studying Fashion Journalism at London College of Fashion.
